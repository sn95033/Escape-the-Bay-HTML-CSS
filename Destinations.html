Escape San Francisco Bay - Where to? How many are moving?

Unsurprisingly, a majority of people move to other Bay area counties, primarily those within a reasonable commute distance to San Francisco. The top 5 counties in CA are directly East and South of San Francisco, and on public transportation routes. Los Angeles county is a bit of a surprise, and housing and opportunities in Los Angeles could explain some of LA's popularity.
