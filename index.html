Escape San Francisco Bay

Summary

The San Francisco County area and Bay area have some of the highest household incomes in the country due to a large number of high-paying tech industries in the city. Yet many people are leaving San Francisco County.

  • How many are leaving? What are the most popular destinations?
  • What are some of the motivations people have to leave ?

    • We have examined several dimensions of the decision to leave San Francisco County, including Social Factors, Incomes, Housing, and Job Opportunities
